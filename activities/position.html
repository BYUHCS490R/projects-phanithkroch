Engagement Questions

  1. What is normal flow in HTML?

    2. Normal flow is the natural top-to-bottom and left-to-right layout of elements without special positioning or layout styles applied

  2. What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

    3. Float move element left or right and lets text wrap around it. Clear stops elements from wrapping around floats and pushes them below.

  3. What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

    4. Relative move from where it normally sits. Fixed Stick to the screen. Absulute escape layout and attach to a parent

    5. To make nice navigation buttons for mobile devices, we focus on touch-friendly design, clear layout, and responsive styling.
